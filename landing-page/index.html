Booking with Wanderlust is easy! You can browse our website for destinations and packages, then either book online or contact our travel consultants for personalized assistance. Our team is available 24/7 to help you plan your perfect trip.

Our packages typically include accommodation, airport transfers, some meals, and selected activities. Each package has different inclusions which are clearly listed on the package details page. We also offer customizable options where you can add or remove components to suit your preferences.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and suppliers involved. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days before departure will receive a full refund minus a small processing fee.
